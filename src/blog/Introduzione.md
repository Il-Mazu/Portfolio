Hi! My name is Marco. I'm an Italian-Polish Computer Science student with a strong passion for music, nerdy stuff, and a bunch of other random interests.

I don't really have a clear plan for this website. I just wanted a place where I could collect everything I enjoy and find interesting. Plus, I kind of stole the idea from a friend of mine (go check them out, they're really cool: [Bianca's corner](https://www.blanche.gay/)).

This whole project started out of boredom during an internship, where I was tasked with learning Ruby, something I wasn't particularly excited about. In general, I've always built websites either for work or out of necessity, without ever really giving myself the chance to be creative and see what I could come up with.

As for writing, it's a skill I'd like to improve, so starting a blog felt like a pretty good idea.
Anyway, I hope you enjoy your stay, and feel free to let me know what you think—once I figure out how to add a comment section, that is.
